    How it Works?

    Our groundbreaking add-on goes above and beyond to assess the environmental impacts of your internet usage. By harnessing the power of these advanced measurement, calculation, and estimation techniques, EcoWatch equips you with the knowledge needed to make informed decisions about your internet usage. Our add-on evaluates the impacts of internet usage by:

  • Measuring the quantity of data transmitted through your internet browser (in bytes).
  • Calculating the electricity consumption associated with this data traffic, using the "1byte" model developed by The Shift Project.
  • Estimating the greenhouse gas emissions resulting from the electricity consumption, based on your selected location.
